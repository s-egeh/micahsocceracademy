About Micah Academy

Our Vision

To be the leading youth soccer academy in our region — producing confident, skilled players and responsible citizens — and to create opportunities for players to progress into professional or collegiate football while promoting community pride.

Our Mission

To nurture and develop young soccer players by providing high-quality training, education, and mentorship.

Core Objectives

  • ✓ Cultivate technical, tactical, physical, and mental skills.
  • ✓ Instill values such as discipline, teamwork, respect, and sportsmanship.
  • ✓ Provide clear pathways for players to reach their full potential.
  • ✓ Promote a healthy lifestyle and inspire a lifelong passion for sport.

Our Programs

Structured training for every age group, focusing on holistic player development from grassroots to elite levels.
